The Path to Independence

1

The Cold Start: Historical Bulk Ingest

Instead of JIT scraping, we pre-populate the database with a massive dataset of historical judgments. Options include open data dumps or a dedicated background scraper for court archives.

2

The Citation Brain: Local Graph Construction

Build an AI-powered citation indexer that scans every judgment locally to detect mentions of other cases, building the "Citing/Cited By" graph without external APIs.

3

The Live Feed: Direct Court Sync

Expand our SupremeCourtScraper (and add others) to pull directly from official cause lists and daily judgment uploads from SCI, High Courts, and e-Courts.
